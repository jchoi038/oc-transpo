Promotional banners

Purpose: Highlight important messages or promotions related to OC Transpo services in a prominent and engaging way.

Guide

Use case scenarios

  • Announcing new or updated transit services
  • Promoting supplementary services such as park-and-ride, bike-and-ride, or accessibility options
  • Directing users to the website for detailed information or schedules

Images & icons

Incorporate relevant images or icons to enhance the message or service logos Provide alt text for images

Content:

  1. text
    • Keep messages concise and action-oriented.
    • Use compelling language to drive engagement.
    • Limit the supplementary text to a maximum of 1-2 sentences.
  2. CTA
    • Use actionable phrases such as “Learn More,” “Explore Services,” or “Get Updates.”
    • Avoid making the text too long and instead hide more descriptive text to be visually hidden

DOs

  • Ensure that the banner title in combination with the Call-to-Action (CTA) provides sufficient context about the banner without requiring additional description. This is crucial for mobile views where descriptions will be hidden
  • Banner titles should be short and to the point, avoid wordiness to maintain consistent height and accommodate mobile display

DONT

  • Overload with information

Banner types

  • Icon banner
  • Horizontal banner
  • Vertical banner
