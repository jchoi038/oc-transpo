Types of monthly permits

Regular permit

{oc_grid_fares:fo-prp}/mo nth

Grants access to permit-required lots, seven days a week, excluding overnight

Permit-required lots:

  • Baseline (before 11 am)
  • Blair (Telesat)
  • Jeanne d’Arc
  • Ray Friel

Gold permit

{oc_grid_fares:fo-gpp}/mo nth

Gold Park & Ride permits guarantee parking seven days a week, excluding overnight (limited availability).

Permits are limited and sold on a first come, first serve basis. Select a parking lot with a gold permit parking lot icon to see if Gold permits are available

Purchasing Permits

To buy a Permit, call 613-842-3777 or email parkandridepermits@ottawa.ca. Permits are on sale between the 15th of the previous month to the 14th of the current month.

Eligibility

To purchase a Park & Ride permit, a valid transit monthly pass for the same month must be purchased. Customers will be asked to produce their current monthly pass to purchase a permit.

Automatic renewal plan for Gold permits

With automatic renewal, you avoid making monthly trips to buy a permit and don’t have to worry about Gold permits being sold out. Here’s how it works:

How to purchase

Call the Gold Pass Administrator at 613-842-3777 to order your monthly permit or Gold permit.

Your permit will be mailed to you automatically before the first of each month.

Tip

The first time you order, call before the 20th day of the month, to allow sufficient time for mailing.

Payment Methods

Pay with a credit card (MasterCard, Visa or American Express). We’ll deduct the cost of your permit each month around the 15th.

How to cancel your automatic renewal plan

You may cancel the automatic deduction any time before the 10th of the preceding month.

Questions about our Park & Ride lots or Permits? Contact our Park & Ride Administrator at 613-842-3777 or parkandridepermits@ottawa.ca.
